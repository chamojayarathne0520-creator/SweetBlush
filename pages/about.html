Our Story

A little bakery with a whole lot of heart

From a home kitchen to your heart

Sweet Blush started as a weekend hobby - a passion project born out of love for baking and a belief that homemade always tastes better. What began as cakes for friends and family quickly grew into something far sweeter.

Every recipe we use has been tested, tweaked, and tasted dozens of times. We use only real butter, fresh eggs, high-quality chocolate, and no shortcuts. When you bite into a Pinkeria cookie or cake, you're tasting years of practice and a genuine love for the craft.

We bake in small batches so every item is as fresh as it can be. Orders are prepared on the day of delivery whenever possible. Because we believe you deserve something that was made just for you — not sitting on a shelf.
